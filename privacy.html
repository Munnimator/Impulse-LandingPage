ImpulseLog ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Apple ID information (when you sign in with Apple)

  • Profile information you choose to add

Financial Data:

  • Impulse purchase amounts you log
  • Savings goals and progress
  • Spending categories and descriptions

Usage Data:

  • App usage statistics
  • Feature interactions

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain app functionality
  • Generate AI-powered insights (Premium users)

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for data in transit
  • Encryption at rest using Firebase Security
  • No data sharing with third parties
  • Regular security audits

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Export your data
  • Delete your account and all data
  • Opt out of AI processing

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. You can delete your account at any time from the app settings.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Database and authentication

Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
