Frequently Asked Questions

Is this just another budget app?

No! ImpulseLog is specifically designed for impulse control, not general budgeting. We focus on the psychology of impulse buying and use gamification to make saving fun and rewarding.

How does the mood tracking work?

You can optionally log your mood when recording an avoided impulse. Our AI analyzes patterns between your emotions and spending urges, helping you understand your triggers.

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption, your data is stored securely in Firebase, and we never share or sell your information. You can export or delete your data anytime.

What makes this different from Mint or YNAB?

ImpulseLog is built specifically for neurodivergent minds with features like quick logging, visual progress, and dopamine-friendly gamification. We're not a full budget app - we're your impulse control companion.

Can I use this with my partner?

Each person needs their own account to track individual progress and achievements. However, you can share victories and compete in community challenges together!
