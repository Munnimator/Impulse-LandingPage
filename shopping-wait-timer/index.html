What the wait timer is for

Delay is not the whole goal. Better decisions are.

Create friction

The timer interrupts the speed of the purchase cycle before the cart becomes an automatic yes.

Track what happened

You can connect the urge, the item, and the outcome, which makes the delay measurable instead of theoretical.

Learn your trigger profile

Over time you can see which moods, categories, or contexts most often need that extra pause.

Workflow

When the urge hits

  • 1Log the item and amount you want to spend
  • 2Apply a wait period before deciding
  • 3Record mood or context if you want deeper pattern tracking

After the timer ends

  • 1Revisit the purchase with less urgency
  • 2Keep the win if you decide not to buy
  • 3Let the result inform future goals, insights, and streaks
